  <h2>ETF Analyzer </h2>

  <p>Currently, the app covers **634 ETFs** across various asset classes. All ETFs are characterized by their identical use of profits: <b>Accumulating</b>. This reflects the preference of mid-term sustainable growth individual retail investors often have.</p>
